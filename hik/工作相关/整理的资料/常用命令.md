 - ==uboot下设置网络参数==
```shell
setenv ipaddr 10.12.99.130;

setenv gatewayip 10.12.99.254;

setenv serverip 10.12.99.43;

saveenv
```

- ==uboot下设置mac==

```shell
setenv ethaddr bc:65:5e:c6:fd:52

saveenv
```

- ==设置网络参数==
```shell
ifconfig eth0 10.12.97.17 netmask 255.255.255.0
```

- ==tftp导入导出==
```shell
tftp -gl filename -r filename 10.12.97.31 -b 8192
tftp -pl filename -r filename 10.12.97.31 -b 8192
```

- ==设置日期==

- ==查看so库是否包含某个函数==
